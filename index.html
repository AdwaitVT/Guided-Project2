Albums Average Attributes Values Over the Decades


Description:

The above bar chart shows all the attributes of the albums that a music accounts of over each deacade. Using the above visualization we can analyze each attribute and see how it has changed over period of decades. Some attributes like acousticness, instrumentalness, liveness, and valence have decrease over period of time. On the other hand the attributes like energy, loudness, speechiness and tempo have increased over the period of time. In a nutshell, from this chart we can guess which factors have influenced the music during the given period of decade.
