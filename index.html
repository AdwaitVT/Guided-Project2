Music Through the Decades

Elizabeth Christman, Rebecca Disipio, Adwait Pradhan, Surendrabikram Thapa

Music is a reflection of the times we live in, and as culture and society changes, so does music. Different eras of music are distinct from each other, and a song released in 2010 has a decidedly different style than a song from the 80s. But what changed? How has music evolved throughout the years? In this data story you will explore the changes in music throughout the decades.

Summary of Results

1960's and 1970's were the most instrumental, lively, and acoustic decades
2000's and 2010's were the most energetic, loud, and speechy decades
These results are the reverse for the "least" categories
Additionally, the 1960's and 1970's are notably the least danceable, and the 2000's and 2010's had the lowest valence scores.
